## üìö Words That Often Confuse / Palabras que Confunden

### Actually vs Currently
- **Actually** = en realidad, de hecho
  - "I thought it was closed, but actually it's open."
- **Currently** = actualmente, ahora
  - "I'm currently studying for my exam."

### Borrow vs Lend
- **Borrow** = pedir prestado (you receive)
  - "Can I borrow your pen?"
- **Lend** = prestar (you give)
  - "Can you lend me your pen?"

### Bring vs Take
- **Bring** = traer (toward speaker)
  - "Bring your book to class tomorrow."
- **Take** = llevar (away from speaker)
  - "Take this letter to the post office."

### Remember vs Remind
- **Remember** = recordar (you do it yourself)
  - "I remember her name."
- **Remind** = recordar a alguien (someone helps you remember)
  - "Please remind me to call."
