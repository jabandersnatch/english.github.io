### **False Friends / Falsos Amigos**
Words that look similar in Spanish but have different meanings:

| English | Spanish Meaning | Spanish False Friend |
|---------|----------------|---------------------|
| actually | en realidad | actualmente = currently |
| embarrassed | avergonzado | embarazada = pregnant |
| sensible | sensato | sensible = sensitive |
| realize | darse cuenta | realizar = to carry out |
| library | biblioteca | librer√≠a = bookstore |
| exit | salida | √©xito = success |

### **Common Confusions:**

**Make vs Do:**
- make: a mistake, progress, money, noise, friends
- do: homework, exercise, housework, a job, your best

**Say vs Tell:**
- say + words: say hello, say sorry
- tell + person: tell me, tell the truth

**See vs Look vs Watch:**
- see: general (see a film)
- look (at): direct attention (look at the board)
- watch: observe over time (watch TV)
