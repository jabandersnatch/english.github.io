## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes / Errores Comunes

### ‚ùå Mistake 1: Wrong structure after despite/in spite of
**Wrong:** Despite I was tired...
**Correct:** Despite being tired... / Despite the fact that I was tired...

### ‚ùå Mistake 2: Using 'because of' + clause
**Wrong:** I stayed home because of I was ill.
**Correct:** I stayed home because I was ill. OR because of my illness.

### ‚ùå Mistake 3: Double connectors
**Wrong:** Although it was raining, but we went out.
**Correct:** Although it was raining, we went out. OR It was raining but we went out.

### ‚ùå Mistake 4: Wrong punctuation
**Wrong:** I was tired so I went home.
**Better:** I was tired, so I went home. OR I was tired. Therefore, I went home.
