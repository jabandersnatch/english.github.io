## ğŸ’¡ Key Strategies / Estrategias Clave

### Before Listening:
1. **Read the notes/form**
   - Predict what type of information is needed
   - Note the topic

2. **Check word limits**
   - How many words can you write?
   - Usually "NO MORE THAN THREE WORDS"

### While Listening:
1. **Write as you listen**
   - Don't wait for the end
   - Use abbreviations if needed

2. **Check spelling**
   - Names especially
   - Numbers clearly

3. **Use second listening**
   - Confirm answers
   - Check spelling
   - Fill missed gaps

### After Listening:
1. **Make sure answers fit grammatically**
   - Read the complete sentence
   - Check it makes sense

2. **Check spelling and numbers**
   - No spelling mistakes!
