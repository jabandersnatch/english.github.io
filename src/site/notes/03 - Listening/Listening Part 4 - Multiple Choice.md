## âš ï¸ Common Traps / Trampas Comunes

### 1. **Changing Mind**
Speaker mentions one thing, then changes:
- "I thought about going to Spain, but I chose Italy instead."
- Answer: Italy (final decision)

### 2. **Other People's Opinions**
Distinguish between:
- What the speaker thinks
- What other people think
- "My friends loved it, but I found it boring."

### 3. **Past vs Future**
- "I wanted to be a teacher" (past - no longer true)
- "I want to be a teacher" (current plan)
